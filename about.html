why design?

my love for creating began in second grade when my childhood friend and i ran over to each other’s houses to make what we called “secret inventions”--rummaging through our garbage and recycling bins, we made contraptions out of scraps to fix everyday problems around our homes. ikea was my version of disneyland. with a mouthwatering $1.00 soft serve in hand, i wandered around the never-ending aisles of furniture, storage cabinets, and containers. laying down on the bean bags, i would stare at the hanging lights in awe of how everything was so simple yet ingenious. though i didn’t know it at the time, looking back this was the root of my love for product design. and...i guess the rest is history (or a story i’d love to share with you!)
